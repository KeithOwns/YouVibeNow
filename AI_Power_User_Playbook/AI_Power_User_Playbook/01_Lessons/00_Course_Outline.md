# AI Power User Playbook - Course Outline

Welcome to the AI Power User Playbook. This course takes you from basic AI interaction to building a fully integrated "Second Brain" AI workflow. *Note: This course is designed as a living document and will be updated regularly to reflect the fast-paced evolution of AI tools.*

Before learning what to do, we must unlearn what *not* to do. Just as learning logical fallacies is the fastest way to learn critical thinking, learning "AI Fallacies" is the fastest way to become an AI power user. See [[00_Primer_AI_Myths]] for the theory.
*   **[[Lesson_00_Primer]]**: The Problem with "Prompt Engineering" and the unintuitive reality of letting go of control.

## Part 1: Context Engineering
Once we've cleared the slate, we must understand the new paradigm of talking to AI. See [[01_Context_Engineering]] for the core concepts.
*   **[[Lesson_01_Context_Engineering]]**: Moving from Prompt Engineering to Context Engineering, Adam's Law, and Thin Prompting.

## Part 2: Establishing the Baseline (ChatGPT Settings)
How to configure the core ChatGPT platform to serve as your reliable foundation. See [[02_The_Baseline_Settings]] to understand why these settings matter.
*   **[[Lesson_02_The_Baseline]]**: The step-by-step guide to configuring Custom Instructions, Memory, Higher Intelligence, and Temporary Chat.

## Part 3: The Digital Twin (Obsidian)
Connecting your baseline AI settings to a local "Second Brain" to achieve true symbiosis. See [[03_Digital_Twin_Theory]] for the philosophy.
*   **[[Lesson_03_Digital_Twin]]**: Setting up Obsidian, the Canvas feature, and integrating your workflows.
