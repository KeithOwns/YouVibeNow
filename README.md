# YouVibeNow (YVB): Autonomous Vibe Coding & Prompt Engineering

Welcome to the definitive curriculum on **Autonomous Vibe Coding**. 

This repository isn't just a course on how to talk to AI. It is a comprehensive framework for structuring AI interactions to be reliable, secure, and infinitely scalable.

## The Core Philosophy: Productionize Your Opinion
The biggest mistake developers make with AI is treating it like a chat interface. They type the same instructions, the same context, and the same safety rules over and over again.

**Stop chatting. Start delegating.**

To build truly autonomous AI workflows, you must *productionize your opinion*. This means taking your coding standards, your architectural preferences, and your safety rules out of your head and encoding them into the file system. When you do this, the AI adopts your "vibe" automatically.

## The 3-Tier Hierarchy
We achieve this through the **3-Tier Hierarchy**, the foundational architecture taught in this course:

1. **Tier 1: Application-Level (The Safety Net)**
   * Your absolute, non-negotiable rules (like InfoSec file backups and baseline identity). These are configured globally in your IDE (e.g., Google Antigravity or Claude Code).
2. **Tier 2: Project-Level (The Folder Architecture)**
   * Your specialized rules for a specific codebase. This is handled by a standard set of markdown files (`AGENTS.md`, `CONTEXT.md`, `MEMORY.md`, `skills/`) living in the root of your project.
3. **Tier 3: Execution-Level (The Thin Prompt)**
   * Your day-to-day chat messages. Because Tier 1 and Tier 2 handle the heavy lifting, your actual chat prompts become incredibly "thin"—only defining the immediate Task and Output Format.
